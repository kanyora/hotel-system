    Notifications

    Just click a button to create a new notification.

    Success notification 

    new Notification('<strong>You</strong> just saved the page <strong>About Us</strong>', 'success');

    Warning notification 

    new Notification('<strong>Bram</strong> left a warning named <strong>Buy this Theme!</strong>', 'warning');

    Just some info 

    new Notification('<strong>Chuck</strong> says he doesn’t see <strong>Modals</strong> yet', 'information');

    An error 

    new Notification('<strong>You</strong> need to look at <strong>Users</strong>', 'error', 'urgent');

    Saved message 

    new Notification('<strong>This</strong> is just another <strong>Informative Message</strong>', 'saved');

    Explanation tabs

    Your secondary menu should look something like this:

    <nav id="secondary">
  <ul>
    <li class="current"><a href="#maintab">Main tab</a></li>
    <li><a href="#secondtab">Explanation tabs</a></li>
    <li><a href="#thirdtab">Optional third tab</a></li>
  </ul>
</nav>

    If you use class="current" on a <li>-tag, that tab will be the default.

    In your content area, you must use the following structure:

    <div class="tab" id="maintab">
</div>

<div class="tab" id="secondtab">
</div>

<div class="tab" id="thirdtab">
</div>
